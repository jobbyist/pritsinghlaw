Quiet Title Actions

When multiple parties claim ownership of the same parcel, a quiet title action operates as a court-issued reset button that wipes away competing claims and delivers a judgment confirming who truly owns the property.

In the Bay Area—where unresolved title defects can freeze million-dollar deals—we guide owners in San Francisco, Oakland, Berkeley, Richmond, Fremont, and beyond through every step of a quiet title lawsuit.

When Is a Quiet Title Action Necessary?

  • Fraudulent or erroneous deeds cloud the chain of title.
  • Boundary or encroachment disputes leave ownership in doubt.
  • Liens, judgments, or inheritance claims threaten a closing.
  • Adverse possession allegations must be resolved before refinance or sale.

Our Process

  • Comprehensive title-chain investigation and document review.
  • Drafting a complaint that complies with CCP § 761.020.
  • Serving every person or entity with a potential interest—miss one and your judgment could be challenged.
  • Recording the final judgment so your ownership is clear and marketable.
