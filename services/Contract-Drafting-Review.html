Why Precise Contracts Matter

Real estate contracts are legally binding instruments governed by the Civil Code and decades of precedent. Ambiguous repair clauses, missing disclosures, or poorly defined contingencies can derail a closing and invite litigation.

  • Purchase-price adjustments & seller credits
  • Inspection, appraisal, and financing contingencies
  • Earnest-money deposit protections
  • Escrow timelines and closing logistics
