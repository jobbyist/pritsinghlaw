Understanding Easements

An easement grants someone limited rights to use land they do not own. Under Civil Code §§ 801–805, easements may be express, implied, or prescriptive.

  • Shared driveways & private roads
  • Utility and access easements
  • Recreational or community-use paths

What Is an Encroachment?

An encroachment is a physical intrusion—like a fence or shed—built over the boundary line. Left unchecked, it can fuel prescriptive easement or adverse-possession claims.
