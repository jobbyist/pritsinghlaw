Your Rights Under California Law

The Homeowner Bill of Rights (Civ. Code §§ 2923.5, 2924.11) restricts “dual-tracking,” mandates early outreach, and requires accurate documentation. If a lender cuts corners, we can halt the sale and seek damages.

  • Incorrect assignments or deed defects
  • Fraudulent or misleading modification offers
