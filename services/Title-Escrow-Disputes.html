What Causes Title & Escrow Disputes?

Title disputes often arise when ownership isn’t clear or there are hidden claims on the property. Escrow disputes occur when funds or documents aren’t handled properly. Common issues include:

  • Liens or Judgments: Unpaid debts, contractor liens, or tax liens that cloud title.
  • Errors in Deeds: Mistakes in legal descriptions or prior transfers.
  • Fraudulent Documents: Forged deeds or improper transfers recorded on title.
  • Escrow Mismanagement: Funds held incorrectly, missed instructions, or misapplied payments.
  • Title Insurance Claims: Denials or disputes with your title insurer when defects are discovered.

Title Insurance Disputes

Title insurance is supposed to protect you from hidden ownership problems, but insurers often push back on coverage. We know how to challenge title insurance denials and make sure your coverage is enforced when it matters most—especially when a defect is discovered after closing.
