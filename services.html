We are fully committed to providing our valued clients with unparalleled legal expertise

Specializing in California real estate law, Pritpal Singh and his team offer unrivaled expertise, ensuring precision, speed, and strategic clarity in every transaction and legal matter.

Real Estate Litigation & Dispute

When negotiations fail, we represent buyers, sellers, landlords, tenants, and developers in a wide range of disputes—from breach of contract to fraud and non-disclosure cases.


We explore mediation and arbitration options early, when appropriate, to resolve conflicts cost-effectively while preserving your commercial relationships and timeline.


Whether it’s enforcing a purchase agreement or challenging encroachments, we bring evidence-backed strategy and a courtroom presence shaped by deep California real estate law experience.

Landlord-Tenant Related Matters

We draft and review leases that clarify responsibilities, minimize liability, and comply with rent control ordinances and disclosure requirements in your jurisdiction.


From nonpayment to nuisance claims, we guide landlords through lawful eviction procedures under California's evolving tenant protection laws.


Whether you're facing lease violations or claims of uninhabitable conditions, we offer fast, practical legal solutions to protect your rights and reputation.

Real Estate Transactions & Closing

We take the time to fully understand our clients' needs and goals, and work tirelessly to negotiate the best terms possible. With our expertise in real estate law and market knowledge, we are able to anticipate potential challenges and mitigate risks. 


When it comes time to close a deal, our attention to detail ensures that all documentation is in order and that the transaction is completed smoothly and efficiently. Our clients can trust that we will always act in their best interests and strive to achieve the most favorable outcome for them.

Deed Transfers & Title Corrections

Our team of experienced professionals is well-versed in the intricacies of real estate law and documentation, ensuring that every document related to the transfer or correction is legally sound and compliant with all relevant laws and regulations.


We work closely with our clients to address any potential issues or discrepancies that may arise during the process.

Easements, Liens & Encumbrances

Navigating utility easements, lien releases, and restrictive covenants requires expert legal care—Prit helps clarify and resolve these complex issues with precision.


In addition to providing expert legal counsel, Prit also offers comprehensive solutions to help clients resolve utility easements, lien releases, and restrictive covenants effectively. 


Whether it involves negotiating with utility companies, obtaining lien releases from creditors, or addressing restrictions in property deeds, Prit's attorneys are committed to finding the best possible outcome for their clients.

Contract Drafting & Review

We draft, analyze, and revise real estate contracts—including purchase agreements, leases, and financing documents—to eliminate ambiguity and protect your legal and financial interests.


Every clause is written with current California statutes and local customs in mind, reducing the risk of disputes or costly enforcement issues down the road.
