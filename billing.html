Pay Your Bill Securely

We make it easy for you to pay your legal fees securely online. Our preferred payment platform, LawPay, is specifically designed for law firms and ensures your payment information is handled with the highest level of security and compliance.

Preferred: LawPay

Our primary payment processor, specifically designed for legal services. LawPay ensures compliance with attorney trust account regulations and provides bank-level security for all transactions.

  • ✓ Accept credit and debit cards securely
  • ✓ eCheck payment options available
  • ✓ Compliant with legal industry standards
  • ✓ Instant payment processing
  • ✓ Detailed payment receipts

Alternative: Stripe

We also accept payments through Stripe as an alternative payment method with the option to set up a payment plan to cover your legal fees using Affirm or Klarna. Contact our support team if you prefer to use this option or need assistance with payment setup.

How to Pay Your Bill:

  1. Click the "Pay Now" button below
  2. You'll be securely redirected to our payment processor
  3. Enter your payment information using credit/debit card or eCheck
  4. Review your payment details
  5. Submit your payment securely
  6. You'll receive a confirmation receipt via email

Security Notice: All payments are processed through encrypted, secure connections. We never store your payment information on our servers. Your payment will open in a secure new tab for your protection.
